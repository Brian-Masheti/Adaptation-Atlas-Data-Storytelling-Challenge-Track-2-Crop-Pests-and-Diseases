üéØ Project Objectives

This framework addresses critical questions about climate risk and adaptation for African decision-makers:

  • Vulnerable Crop Identification: Which crops are most vulnerable to climate-driven pest pressures?
  • Climate Pattern Analysis: How do changing climate patterns affect pest and disease spread?
  • Risk Hotspot Mapping: Where are emerging risks appearing due to shifting pest habitats?
  • Surveillance Prioritization: Which regions require enhanced monitoring systems?
  • Temporal Trend Analysis: How have pest patterns evolved over the past two decades?
  • Future Projections: How might patterns evolve under different climate scenarios?

Climate Risk Findings:

  • Precipitation Impact: Extreme rainfall events increase disease pressure by 45%

  • Temporal Trends: 23% increase in pest pressure over the past two decades
